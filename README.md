VIWI - AutoLogin is an Anti-DDoS module that will store the details of your last-known active character and attempt to automatically reconnect to them in the event of a sudden disconnect.

Additionally, AutoLogin prevents your client from killing itself on any lobby/disconnection errors, and offers client launching functionality during detected AuthenticationErrors/Expired Auth Tokens.
